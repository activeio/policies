Privacy Policy

Mantra Jaap · Last updated 10 June 2026

Mantra Jaap is a digital mala for counting jaaps. It is built to be private by default — your chanting counts and history live on your device, not on our servers. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles and why.

Data stored on your device

Your mantras, daily jaap counts, malas, streaks, and preferences (language, theme) are stored locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to us, and it is removed when you uninstall the app.

Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob. To deliver ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and use device and advertising identifiers. By default the app requests non‑personalized ads.

Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can read how Google uses information from apps that use its services at Google's partner policy page. On Android, you can reset or delete your advertising ID under Settings → Google → Ads.

Information we do not collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your contacts, location, photos, or files
  • Any personally identifying information

We do not sell your data to anyone.

In short: the app keeps your sadhana private on your device. The only data leaving your phone is what Google AdMob needs to show ads.

Children's privacy

Mantra Jaap is intended for a general audience (ages 13+) and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any material change will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
